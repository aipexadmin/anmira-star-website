About Anmira Star

Anmira Star Co., Ltd. is a cutting-edge AI media production and talent agency headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.

We believe AI doesn't replace creativity — it amplifies it. From AI-generated short films, commercials, and music videos to virtual idol management and celebrity branding, we are redefining the boundaries of visual storytelling.

"Anmira" — a fusion of Anima (soul) and Mira (miracle). Our name reflects our mission: to breathe soul into AI-born miracles.
